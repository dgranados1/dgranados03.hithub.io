Weekend Events

At Dave’s Bed & Breakfast, weekends are all about relaxation, connection, and embracing the warm spirit of Hawaiʻi. Whether you're here to unwind or explore, we offer a variety of themed activities designed to make your stay unforgettable.

Aloha Friday Welcome Gathering

Kick off your weekend with our Aloha Friday tradition! Guests enjoy light pupus (snacks), tropical drinks, and a friendly meet-and-greet on the lanai. It’s the perfect way to ease into vacation mode.

Live Island Music Nights

Every Saturday evening, we host live Hawaiian-style music featuring ukulele, slack-key guitar, or island acoustic sets. Relax under the string lights and enjoy the soothing sounds of Hawaiʻi.

Tropical Breakfast Bar

On Saturdays and Sundays, we expand our breakfast bar with fresh island favorites—pineapple, mango, guava pastries, coconut-topped waffles, and Kona coffee.

Sunset Fire Pit & S’mores

Enjoy a cozy evening around our outdoor fire pit. We provide s’mores kits with a tropical twist—coconut chocolate, macadamia nut bars, and more.

Optional Weekend Activities

We partner with local instructors and guides to offer optional adventures: Beginner surf lessons, Tidepool and beach walks, Yoga on the lawn, Guided waterfall hikes.

Sunday Morning Relaxation

End your weekend with a peaceful morning featuring mellow Hawaiian music, fresh fruit-infused water, and a calm atmosphere to prepare for the week ahead.
