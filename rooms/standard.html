Welcome to Our Standard Room!

Our Standard Room gives you the best value for your money. While not as luxurious as our Express Room, it still offers excellent comfort and all the essentials – perfect for travelers who want a clean, quiet, and inviting place to relax.

Price

The Standard Room is priced at $129 per night – an affordable option that doesn’t compromise on quality or comfort.

Amenities

  • Comfortable queen-size bed
  • Flat-screen TV with streaming services
  • Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi
  • Private bathroom with fresh towels
  • Coffee maker & mini-fridge
  • Heating and air conditioning
  • Free parking & daily housekeeping

Why Guests Love It

Guests love the Standard Room for its excellent value, cozy atmosphere, and reliable amenities. It’s clean, quiet, and consistently delivers a comfortable and stress-free stay.
